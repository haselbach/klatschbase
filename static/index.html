Welcome to Klatschbase v0.5.1.
Click on a client or room to send a message. Click on the ☐ next to a room to subscribe. Click on the ☑ next to a room to unsubscribe.
You can also send direct messages to other users by entering /msg WHO MSG and to rooms by entering /msg #ROOM MSG.
You can subscribe to a room with /join #ROOM and unsubscribe with /part #ROOM. The # is optional. Regular expressions are supported.
